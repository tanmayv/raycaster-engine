Covid19
The purpose of this auction is to raise funds for Covid19 relief. It is a non profit auction and the amount would go to the following trusts:
  • PM Care Fund (https://www.pmindia.gov.in/en/about-pm-cares-fund/)
  • Rahat Goonj (https://goonj.org/support-covid-19-affected/)
  • Trust of your choice working for Covid! (can be even located outside India, incase of bidders from abroad)

Kamayani Sharma

I am an amateur artist who eschews showcasing paintings publicly. My paintings are majorly driven by my mood of the hour and memories from the past. I personally feel melancholy engenders aesthetic richness to art and life. Despite my several attempts of refrainment, the blues, therefore, always leave a shade in my art work. The sole purpose of this auction is to raise funds to deal with the distress caused by COVID19 and will be a non profit auction. For my friends located outside India, the contribution would go to your respective countries.
